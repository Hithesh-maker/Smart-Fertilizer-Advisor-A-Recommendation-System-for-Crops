Key Parameters used by SSNM

SSNM evaluates soil nutrients, crop requirements, environmental factors, and moisture to produce precise fertilizer recommendations.

N — Nitrogen

Essential for vegetative growth, leaf development, and chlorophyll production.

P — Phosphorus

Promotes root development, early growth, and flower/fruit formation.

K — Potassium

Enhances stress tolerance, water regulation, and overall plant vigor.

Moisture

Critical for nutrient uptake and metabolic activities in plants.

Temperature

Influences nutrient cycling and microbial activity in soil.

Humidity

High humidity can increase disease pressure, low humidity affects transpiration.

Soil Type

Determines water retention, nutrient availability, and root penetration.

Output

Recommended fertilizers include Urea, DAP, 10-26-26, and micronutrient blends.
